Featured Tourist Attractions

Victoria Memorial

A magnificent marble monument dedicated to Queen Victoria, showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture and housing a fascinating museum of British Raj artifacts.

Historical Museum Architecture

Sundarbans National Park

The world's largest mangrove forest and home to the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger. A UNESCO World Heritage Site offering unique wildlife experiences.

Wildlife UNESCO Site Mangroves

Darjeeling

The "Queen of Hills" famous for its tea gardens, stunning Himalayan views, and the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway toy train.

Hill Station Tea Gardens Toy Train

Kalimpong

A serene hill station known for its Buddhist monasteries, flower nurseries, and breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga mountain range.

Monasteries Flowers Mountain Views

Ayodhya Hills, Purulia

A picturesque destination in Purulia, famous for its rugged hills, lush forests, sparkling waterfalls, and rich tribal culture, offering a perfect mix of adventure and tranquility.

Scenic Adventurous Cultural

Digha

A popular seaside town along the Bay of Bengal, known for its golden beaches, gentle waves, vibrant seafood stalls, and stunning sunrise and sunset views.

Coastal Lively Relaxing
