Victoria Memorial

About Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial in Kolkata is a stunning white-marble building built in honor of Queen Victoria. Surrounded by lush gardens, it is one of the city's most iconic landmarks and a major tourist attraction.

Architecture

A unique blend of British and Mughal styles with intricate marble carvings.

Museum

Home to rare paintings, manuscripts, and artifacts from the British era.

Best Time to Visit

Winter months (November–February) offer pleasant weather for sightseeing.
