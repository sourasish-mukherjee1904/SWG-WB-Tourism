Ayodhya Hills, Purulia

About Ayodhya Hills, Purulia

Ayodhya Hills in Purulia, West Bengal, is a serene hill range known for its breathtaking landscapes, waterfalls, and tribal culture. It is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Natural Beauty

Rolling hills, dense forests, and beautiful lakes create a peaceful atmosphere.

Activities

Trekking, rock climbing, and photography are popular among visitors.

Local Culture

Experience the unique traditions of local tribal communities.
